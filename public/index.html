Welcome!

Welcome to the internet home of The Compass Rose Band, based out of Cedar Rapids, IA.

We are a Classic / Southern rock band that plays high-energy music from artists such as:

  • The Eagles
  • The Beatles
  • Zac Brown
  • Styx
  • Lynyrd Skynyrd
  • Merle Haggard
  • Vince Gill
  • Waylon Jennings, and more.
